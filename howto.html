How to Play:



Goal: Reach the "end goal"-- the final marker-- on the "Street View" screen on the right.


1. Select a level:
  • Fenway: From the marked start position, reach the end marker at Boston's Fenway Park.
  • Here: From your marked current location, reach the end marker.
  • Easy: Start and end location are marked on the map. Yellow avatar shows your current location.
  • Medium: Start and end location are marked on the map. No yellow avatar.
  • Hard: Only end location is marked on the map. No yellow avatar or start marker.


2. As the timer starts, navigate using the arrows on the righhand "Street View" screen to the end location.

3. Race the clock and try to reach the end marker in the fastest time possible.

Quickest time= highest score!
